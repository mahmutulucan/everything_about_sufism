Who are we?

We are a platform rooted in scientific inquiry and open-mindedness. Our mission is to curate a comprehensive content archive covering all aspects of Sufism, fostering an environment of respectful discussion around these topics.

So, how can we achieve this?

This will not be accomplished by us, but by you, our users, who become part of the 'Everything About Sufism' community. In other words, this platform will truly embody its name through the contributions of users like you, who join our global family.

What are our boundaries?

On this platform, no one will be silenced because of their opinions. While we embrace 'free thought,' insults and hate speech are strictly prohibited. Therefore, while we encourage open dialogue, disrespect is not tolerated.
